About Me

Hello! I am currently a Ph.D. in The Integrated Science and Engineering Project at the National University of Singapore. I am conducting research under the supervision of Prof. T. H. Lee (Fellow of the Singapore Academy of Engineering) in Lee Group.

I am a researcher driven by a passion for solving foundational challenges at the intersection of artificial intelligence and physical sciences. My work in AI for Science (AI4S) and AI for Engineering (AI4E) focuses on building intelligent systems that can understand, predict, and optimize complex real-world phenomena, leveraging advanced models like Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) and Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs).

My research philosophy centers on bridging rigorous academic theory with tangible industrial impact. This is reflected in my first-author publications in multiple SCI-Q1 journals, and complemented by hands-on experience developing cutting-edge AI solutions at industry leaders like Huawei, JD.com (mentor: Hui Shen), and Apple as an algorithm engineer.

This pursuit is built on a foundation of consistent academic excellence. I was honored to be named an Outstanding Graduate (Top 1%) and graduate ranked first in my grade from both my Master‚Äôs program at Tsinghua University and my undergraduate studies at the Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT).
